Inspired by a Smile I Haven’t Stopped Thinking About
Not everything beautiful needs a reason.
Hi, I know you don’t really know me — who I am or why I’m sending this message. And honestly, that’s okay. It’s not a big thing… because I’m sure many people admire you after seeing you. Some even end up bothering you. I just hope I don’t become one of those troubles. That’s why I’ve always admired you quietly, from a distance. I don’t even know if this message will reach you… but if it does, maybe destiny wanted it to. It’s been one year since I first saw you. I still remember that day so clearly. I wish I had said something then… but you looked so beautiful that I completely forgot how to speak. I was literally standing there holding a rose, and you were sitting in that Sador Mekhela… you looked absolutely breathtaking. Since that day, I haven’t been able to forget you. I don’t know why… but it feels like something settled quietly inside my heart and decided not to leave. As a stranger, I’ve only known what I could from a distance — your likes, your dislikes, the way you care about your people… especially the way you love them. That’s something really rare and beautiful. I just wished I could know you more closely — not to disturb your peace, but maybe to be a small part of your smile. Recently, I noticed you’ve started writing beautiful poems. I didn’t know that before… but now I think the words you write are as graceful as you are. Anyway, if anything I said feels awkward or uncomfortable, please forgive me. That was never my intention. And before I end this… let me confess something funny — I practiced this message in my head at least 50 times, but I’m still nervous while sending it. So if this sounds dramatic, just smile a little and blame it on my poor heart. Maybe someday, if you feel like it, I’d love to know — What’s something that always makes you genuinely happy? Have a beautiful day. 🌸 — Someone who once forgot how to speak because of you. 🌸
